Why Maasmechelen?

rich in history and nature

A visit to Maasmechelen begins at the Visit Maasmechelen visitor center. Let the staff inspire and inspire you. They will be happy to show you around the region. Moreover, you will find delicious regional products, walking and cycling maps, souvenirs and more. So be sure to step inside. You are most welcome.
